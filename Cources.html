Intermediate

An intermediate course typically refers to a level of study that builds on basic knowledge and skills, aiming to deepen understanding and proficiency in a particular subject. These courses are designed for learners who have already grasped the fundamentals and are ready to tackle more complex concepts and applications. Intermediate courses can be found in various fields, including language learning, technical skills, and academic subjects

Industrial training(ITI)

An Industrial Training Institute (ITI) is a government-run institution in India that provides vocational training to students. Established under the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, ITIs offer courses in various technical and non-technical fields. These courses are designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge required for specific trades, making them job-ready.

  1. Engineering
  2. Non-Engineering

Integrated Engineering(IIT)

The Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT), also known as IIIT, is a group of institutions in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, India. These institutions are focused on providing high-quality education in information technology and related fields.
